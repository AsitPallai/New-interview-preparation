Modern Air Brake System — Stepwise Air Flow

  1. Air Generation & Cleaning
    • Engine drives Air Compressor → compresses atmospheric air.
    • Compressed air goes to the Air Dryer (removes moisture, oil, dust).
    • Then air is stored in the Purge Tank for regeneration of dryer.

  2. Distribution of Air
    • From the dryer, air goes to the Quick Service Protection Valve (QSPV).
    • QSPV distributes air to:
      • Front Air Tank
      • Rear Air Tank
      • Parking/Accessory Air Tank
    • This ensures that if one circuit fails, the others remain functional (safety feature).

  3. Brake Application System
    • From front & rear air tanks, air goes to the Dual Brake Valve (Foot Brake Valve).
    • When the driver presses the brake pedal:
      • The Dual Brake Valve sends controlled air pressure to Brake Chambers via sensing and delivery lines.
      • Quick Release Valve (QRV)is used for fast release of air from chambers, so brakes release quickly.
